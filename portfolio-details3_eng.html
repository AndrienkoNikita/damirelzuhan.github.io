Magazine Cover

I created this cover fairly quickly. First, I found a suitable stock photo for a magazine cover, then I thought about what kind of magazine this image could belong to. I decided it would work well as a cover for a collage-style magazine featuring beautiful photos inside. Since this type of magazine would have minimal text, I kept the cover text simple to match the clean interior. I created everything in Adobe Photoshop for convenience, then transferred it exactly as-is to Adobe InDesign.
