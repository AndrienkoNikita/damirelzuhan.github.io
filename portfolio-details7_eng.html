Draw Me a Song

The goal of this project was to create an illustration inspired by a song. I chose Clint Eastwood by Gorillaz. Based on the song title, I immediately decided that the main character in my illustration would be a cowboy. There’s also a line in the song that says, "I’ve got the sunshine in a bag," which inspired me to add a bag of sunlight on the character’s back. I also included the song’s lyrics in the background. Overall, I think it turned out to be a great illustration.
