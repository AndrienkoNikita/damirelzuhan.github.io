Wine Label

This was a very quick project that I started at the last minute. With the help of ChatGPT, I came up with a name for the wine brand. I then created a quick association for the name "Luna Vitus." It's a simple Latin-style name that resembles the word "luna," meaning moon. With this in mind, I designed a logo for my brand, created it in Adobe Illustrator, and placed everything on a mockup.
