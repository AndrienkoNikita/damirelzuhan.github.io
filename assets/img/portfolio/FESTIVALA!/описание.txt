Festivala!

For the school design project "Festivala!", I developed a cohesive visual identity and created a series of materials for the festival. Everything was designed in a consistent and recognizable style to give the event a unified and professional look.

My contributions included:

(тут список)
A poster that captures the festival’s atmosphere.
Merchandise – mugs and a T-shirt featuring a unique design.
A functional website, developed in collaboration with ICT student Noah Loeff.
An A5 map, helping visitors easily navigate the venue.
An A5 schedule, handed out at the entrance for a well-organized festival experience.
(тут конец списка)
For this project, I worked with Adobe Photoshop, Adobe Illustrator, Adobe InDesign, Figma, and Autodesk Maya (for the 3D logo). The unified design approach ensured that all elements came together to create a strong and professional festival brand.
